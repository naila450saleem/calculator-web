Understanding Food Calories

Calories are a measure of energy. The average adult needs about 2000-2500 calories per day to maintain their weight, but this varies based on age, gender, weight, height, and activity level.

What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to accomplish its most basic (basal) life-sustaining functions at complete rest. This includes breathing, circulation, cell production, and nutrient processing.
